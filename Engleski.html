Detailed description

Welcome to Vila Knez - your oasis of peace, located along the beautiful coast of the city of Primošten, which will provide you with a unique experience.

Our apartments, located in Tepli bok bay, were created with one goal in mind: to make your vacation an unforgettable moment.

Located in the first row to the sea, along a beautiful promenade through a pine forest that stretches all the way to the very center of Primošten. In addition to comfortable beach chairs and umbrellas, our dedicated staff will take care of your satisfaction and comfort, ensuring that your vacation is unforgettable.

Villa Knez is open all year round and offers a perfect holiday in all seasons. The mild climate provides a pleasant sea temperature that will allow you to enjoy both the pre-season and the post-season.

Do not hesitate - book your stay at Vila Knez; we guarantee you won't regret it. Your adventure starts here!
